# 1. What is correlation? 
- Correlation measures the relationship between two variables:
- It shows:
   - How they move together
   - Strength and direction of the relationship
- Value range: -1 to +1
- Types of correlation
  - Positive - Both increase together 
  - Negative - One increases, other decreases
  - No Correlation - No relationship

---
# 2. Difference between positive and negative correlation

| Type          | Meaning                        | Example                 |
| ------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| Positive (+1) | Both increase together         | Study hours ↑ → Marks ↑ |
| Negative (-1) | One increases, other decreases | Stress ↑ → Productivity ↓      |

---

# 3. What is heatmap? 
- A heatmap is a graphical representation of data where values are shown using colors.
- Used to visualize correlation matrix
- Dark/bright colors indicate strong relationships

---

# 4. What is pairplot? 
Pairplot shows relationships between multiple variables together. It’s useful for exploring multivariate data.

---
# 5. Why correlation is important in ML? 
- Helps in feature selection
- Removes redundant variables
- Improves model performance
- Detects multicollinearity
- Reduce overfitting
