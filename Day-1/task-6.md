## Practical Thinking Task

Think and write:  
"If you build an AI project, what problem will you solve and how?"

If I build an AI project, I would focus on solving the problem of "fake job postings on online job portals". Many job seekers, especially freshers, find it difficult to identify whether a job is genuine or a scam. This can lead to wasted time, loss of personal information, and sometimes financial fraud.

To solve this, I would develop an "AI-based system that can detect fake job listings". The system would analyze job descriptions, salary details, company information, and contact methods using machine learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP). It would identify patterns such as unrealistic salaries, vague descriptions, or missing company details.

The model would be trained on a dataset containing both real and fake job postings. Based on this, it would classify a job as real or fake and provide a confidence score.
I would implement this using Python, Scikit-learn, and Flask to create a simple web interface. This project would help users make safer decisions and reduce the risk of job-related scams.
